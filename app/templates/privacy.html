Data Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share data only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help deliver our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Security Incidents: With law enforcement when investigating cybersecurity threats
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)
